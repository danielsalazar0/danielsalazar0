### 🎧 About Me

I'm a **Music Informatics** student at the **University of Milan (UniMi)** with a deep passion for the intersection of **code and sound**. I love combining software development with audio signal processing and music production — always looking for new ways to apply technology to creativity.

- 🔭 **Currently working on:** Producing music for artists across various genres, and exploring audio classification scripts
- 🌱 **Currently learning:** Advanced C++, Go, and MATLAB applied to audio & DSP
- ✈️ **Next chapter:** Erasmus at **Universidad Complutense de Madrid (UCM)** — 2026/2027 🇪🇸
